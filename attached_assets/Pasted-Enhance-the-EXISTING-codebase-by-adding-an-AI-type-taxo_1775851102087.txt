Each category page should include:

## 1. Intro section

* what the category is
* what users use it for

## 2. Best options section

Show 2–3 options such as:

* best overall
* cheapest viable
* best for a specific use case

Use existing affiliate/config/recommendation data where possible.

## 3. Pricing pattern section

Explain key pricing considerations for this category.

Examples:

* coding AI may justify premium spend for debugging
* writing AI often has cheaper viable options
* support AI benefits from lower-cost high-volume choices

## 4. Related comparisons

Link to existing comparison pages relevant to that category.

## 5. Related best lists

Link to existing best-list pages if available.

## 6. Calculator CTA

Include a clear CTA into the calculator or decision engine.

## 7. FAQ

Include category-specific FAQs if your existing page system supports it.
