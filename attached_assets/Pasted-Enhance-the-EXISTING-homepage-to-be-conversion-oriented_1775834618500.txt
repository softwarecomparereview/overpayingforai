## Part 2: Remove fake affordances

### Problem

Some UI elements look clickable but do nothing.

### Fix

Audit all:

* cards
* highlighted sections
* feature boxes

For each:

* either make clickable (anchor scroll or link)
* OR remove clickable styling (cursor, hover, shadow)

No element should feel interactive without action.
